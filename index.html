About

I started playing the bass when I was 12 years old and haven’t looked back. Though I didn’t realise it at the time I had always enjoyed listening to the bass lines. For my first 5 years I had lessons from professional bass player Laurence Canty. He taught me the important fundamentals of bass playing. Since then, I have continued my development by playing along with CDs of my favourite bands, playing along with the radio, and jamming with local musicians and friends.

In 2009 I created the YouTube channel BedroomBassist - the name that came from a friend joking that I had not been in a band for a while. With my bass covers, tutorials and product reviews over the last five years I have amassed 1.3 million views. More importantly to me I now have a following of 4,000+ awesome subscribers on Youtube, 1,500+ on Facebook and 500+ on Twitter.

My subscription numbers grew rapidly in 2012 when I won the Sandberg Guitars Contest with the song ‘Hammerhead’ from my solo EP, ‘Can’t Sleep In Silence’. I won a signed and one-of-a-kind original prototype of a signature bass. It was built for Thomas “MarloweDK” Risell and it still blows my mind every day when I look at it. I am still overwhelmed by the support I got - and still continue to get - for my submission.

Today I have two main projects. The one that keeps me the busiest is working with a great singer/songwriter called Molly Warburton. I’ve been working with her since 2013 along with talented guitarist Luke Wilding. Together we write songs and have played over 150 gigs each year since I joined. Her debut album** is on the way in early 2015 and we’re very excited about it. Alongside this project is my solo instrumental, bass-lead rock sound in which I play intertwined bass lines and melodies. My EP is available online now and I have an album in the works.
